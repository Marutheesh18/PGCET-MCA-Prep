Mocktest Guidelines

  1. Parents are expected to co-operate with the school authorities in enforcing regularity, punctuality, and discipline in their wards and by taking a daily interest in their children's progress.
  2. Parents can meet the Principal between 8.00 a.m. and 9.00 a.m. or between 3.30 p.m. and 4.30 p.m. on all working days.
  3. Parents are requested to meet the teachers occasionally to discuss the progress of their wards.
  4. Rank cards should be checked and duly signed by the parents within the time prescribed.
  5. Students who copy in the examination will be issued a T.C. at the end of the academic year.
  6. Students who play truant, i.e., roam about without attending school, will also be issued a T.C. at the end of the academic year.
